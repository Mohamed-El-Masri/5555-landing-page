Key Programs & Services

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy

Evidence-based therapy focusing on improving specific behaviors such as communication, social skills, and learning abilities.

Sensory Integration Activities

Techniques that help children process and respond to sensory information effectively.

Motor Skills Development

Activities to improve fine and gross motor coordination essential for daily functioning.

Social Interaction Training

Structured programs to develop social skills and strengthen peer relationships.

Educational Support

Personalized academic assistance to help children succeed in educational settings.

Cognitive Growth

Activities to enhance thinking, reasoning, and problem-solving abilities.
